Monopoly

Tencent's emergence as a major games manufacturer is impressive. With roots in social media, entertainment and technology, the company has made a name for itself in the video game industry. Their influence comes not only from their ownership of gaming companies, but also from their investments and partnerships. They have secured large stakes in game developers around the world, not just in China. Tencent's influence goes beyond money; the company is an expert in technology, data and user behavior and shapes the gaming experience. They use their huge user base and access to data to improve game design, distribution, and the way games make money.

They are also big in esports, sponsoring tournaments and promoting the visibility of games. But some fear Tencent's dominance could harm competition and innovation in gaming, which could impact developers and gamers. Nevertheless, Tencent's influence is enormous. From game development to esports and technology, Tencent is a major player thanks to its investments, technical capabilities and strategy. Their influence on global gaming is undeniable.
